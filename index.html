About me

I had always known as a teenager that I would be a Web Developer but did not take the traditional route to study it at the university. By the age of 15 I used my father's computer not to play solitaire but attempting to clone the BBC and CNN websites and failing horribly. In my early twenties I was accepted for an undergraduate degree to study Business Administration at Laurea University of Applied Sciences.

But Studying computer related subjects has always been my passion so during my undergraduate studies I excelled at software related modules more than anything else and I did my best to add technology subjects to my syllabus for a BBA list of courses. Spending time reading "The Dummies" books especially on computer related subjects has always been the way of passing time for me, my favourites being DOS for Dummies and PC's for Dummies.

My goals are to become a full-stack web developer capable of handling both the front and backend of the software stack and being able to handle different technologies comfortably.
